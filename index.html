Overview

This site serves as a platform for hosting our collected data resources in the fight against COVID-19. We are releasing a Twitter data, selected based on COVID-19. We include geolocation and keywords annotations. These resources are focused on social media data and how it can be used to prevent the spread of COVID-19. Possible applications include the combating of misinformation, supporting messaging from public health organizations and tracking information about the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.
